FAQ-001 / Common Questions

Node Group
FAQs

What does Node Group do?+

Node Group is a specialist scaffold design consultancy. We design scaffolding, hoists and mast climbing work platforms (MCWPs) for construction projects across the UK and Ireland. We produce 2D construction drawings, full 3D models and structural calculations on every project. We do not erect or hire scaffolding — we are a design consultancy only.

Where does Node Group operate?+

We are based in Walthamstow, London and serve clients across the entire United Kingdom and Ireland. We work with scaffold contractors, main contractors and developers on projects ranging from central London heritage buildings to large-scale commercial developments across the UK and Dublin.

Do you provide 3D models?+

Yes. Full 3D models are issued as standard on every project — not as an optional extra. This gives everyone on site complete clarity before the first tube is lifted, and allows for clash detection with the permanent structure that 2D drawings alone cannot provide.

What is a CAT 3 design check?+

A CAT 3 check is the highest level of independent design verification in UK temporary works. It involves a fully independent engineer reviewing the design, calculations and drawings to confirm they are safe and compliant. Node Group provides fully insured CAT 3 checking across Network Rail and other industry frameworks.

Can you design scaffolding for listed buildings?+

Yes. Heritage scaffold design is a core part of our work. We have designed scaffolding for Grade I listed buildings including the Royal Albert Hall and LSO St Luke's Church, Grade II* listed buildings including Woolwich Town Hall, and protected structures in Ireland including Garda HQ in Phoenix Park, Dublin. We specialise in freestanding solutions where ties into historic fabric are not permitted.

Who do you typically work with?+

We work primarily with scaffold contractors, main contractors and developers. You speak to an engineer from the first call — not a sales desk. Our clients include Unison Scaffolding, Proplant Scaffolding, UP Scaffolding and Ainscaff Scaffolding among others.
