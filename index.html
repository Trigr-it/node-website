SVC-001 / What We Do

Every Service You Need.
Nothing You Don't.

From bespoke scaffold design to fully independent CAT 3 checking — full structural calculations included as standard on every project.

SVC / 01 — Scaffold
Bespoke Scaffold Design

Fully tailored designs built around your site. Complex geometry, restricted access, heritage constraints — we design for the project you have, not an idealised version of it.

SVC / 02 — Hoist & MCWP
Hoist & MCWP Design

Mast climbing work platforms and construction hoists designed to current standards. Full structural interface with the permanent structure included on every project.

SVC / 03 — 2D & 3D
2D & 3D Design Output

Detailed 2D construction drawings and full 3D models — issued together as standard. Every party on site has complete clarity before the first tube is lifted.

SVC / 04 — Checking
CAT 3 Design Check

Fully insured independent checking across Network Rail and other frameworks. We check it like our name's on it — because after sign-off, it is.

3D-001 / Our Differentiator

Full 3D Models.
Every Single Job.

Most scaffold design firms issue 2D drawings and call it done. We deliver detailed 2D drawings alongside a complete 3D model on every project — regardless of complexity or size. It's not an add-on. It's how we work.

No ambiguity on site

Everyone sees exactly what's being built before they start. Fewer RFIs, fewer delays, fewer phone calls.

Clash detection built in

3D models surface interface conflicts with the permanent structure that 2D drawings miss entirely.

Better client sign-off

Main contractors and clients review and approve the complete design before a tube goes up.

PROC-001 / How It Works

From Brief to
Buildable Design

A simple process built around getting you a complete, construction-ready package — without unnecessary back-and-forth.

01
Initial Brief

Tell us the project — site constraints, scaffold type, programme. We ask the right questions from the start so the design process runs smoothly.

02
Site Visit

For complex projects, one of our engineers attends site to review conditions in full before a single line is drawn.

ABT-003 / How We Work

The Node Standard

STD / 01
Specialist Focus

Temporary works design is what we do. Not a department within a larger practice. Every person here is an expert in this specific discipline — not a generalist covering multiple areas.

STD / 02
Calculations First

Every design is backed by full structural calculations and frame analysis. If you can't show your working, the design isn't finished. We don't issue anything we can't defend in detail.

STD / 03
Clear for Site

2D drawings and full 3D models issued together on every project. The person building it should never have to interpret an ambiguous drawing. If it's unclear, it isn't done.

ABT-004 / History

How Node Was Built

Founded
Node Group Established

Founded in London by engineers who believed temporary works design deserved the same rigour as permanent structures. Began with a small team and a conviction that the industry could do better — clearer drawings, more robust calculations, and faster turnaround without shortcuts.

Growth
Ireland Operations Begin

Expanded into the Irish market, building strong relationships with scaffold contractors and main contractors across Dublin and the wider country. Established a solid track record operating across both UK and Irish regulatory frameworks.

Capability
Full 3D Design Across the Team

Invested in full 3D modelling capability across the entire team. From this point, every project is issued with both detailed 2D construction drawings and a complete 3D model — giving everyone involved full clarity before a tube is lifted.

Today
Node Group — Saxon House, London E17

Operating from Walthamstow with a team of specialist engineers delivering scaffold, hoist and MCWP design across heritage, commercial, residential and infrastructure sectors throughout the UK and Ireland.
