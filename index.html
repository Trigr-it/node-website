SVC-001 / What We Do

Every Service You Need.
Nothing You Don't.

From bespoke scaffold design to fully independent CAT 3 checking — full structural calculations included as standard on every project.

SVC / 01 — Scaffold
Bespoke Scaffold Design

Fully tailored designs built around your site. Complex geometry, restricted access, heritage constraints — we design for the project you have, not an idealised version of it.

SVC / 02 — Hoist & MCWP
Hoist & MCWP Design

Mast climbing work platforms and construction hoists designed to current standards. Full structural interface with the permanent structure included on every project.

SVC / 03 — 2D & 3D
2D & 3D Design Output

Detailed 2D construction drawings and full 3D models — issued together as standard. Every party on site has complete clarity before the first tube is lifted.

SVC / 04 — Checking
CAT 3 Design Check

Fully insured independent checking across Network Rail and other frameworks. We check it like our name's on it — because after sign-off, it is.

3D-001 / Our Differentiator

Full 3D Models.
Every Single Job.

Most scaffold design firms issue 2D drawings and call it done. We deliver detailed 2D drawings alongside a complete 3D model on every project — regardless of complexity or size. It's not an add-on. It's how we work.

No ambiguity on site

Everyone sees exactly what's being built before they start. Fewer RFIs, fewer delays, fewer phone calls.

Clash detection built in

3D models surface interface conflicts with the permanent structure that 2D drawings miss entirely.

Better client sign-off

Main contractors and clients review and approve the complete design before a tube goes up.

PROC-001 / How It Works

From Brief to
Buildable Design

A simple process built around getting you a complete, construction-ready package — without unnecessary back-and-forth.

01
Initial Brief

Tell us the project — site constraints, scaffold type, programme. We ask the right questions from the start so the design process runs smoothly.

02
Site Visit

For complex projects, one of our engineers attends site to review conditions in full before a single line is drawn.

03
Design & Calculations

Full design package — 2D drawings, 3D model and structural calculations included as standard on every project.

04
Issue & Support

Design issued for construction. We stay available to the site team for queries and clarifications throughout the build.
