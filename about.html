ABT-003 / How We Work

The Node Standard

STD / 01
Specialist Focus

Temporary works design is what we do. Not a department within a larger practice. Every person here is an expert in this specific discipline — not a generalist covering multiple areas.

STD / 02
Calculations First

Every design is backed by full structural calculations and frame analysis. If you can't show your working, the design isn't finished. We don't issue anything we can't defend in detail.

STD / 03
Clear for Site

2D drawings and full 3D models issued together on every project. The person building it should never have to interpret an ambiguous drawing. If it's unclear, it isn't done.

ABT-004 / History

How Node Was Built

Founded
Node Group Established

Founded in London by engineers who believed temporary works design deserved the same rigour as permanent structures. Began with a small team and a conviction that the industry could do better — clearer drawings, more robust calculations, and faster turnaround without shortcuts.

Growth
Ireland Operations Begin

Expanded into the Irish market, building strong relationships with scaffold contractors and main contractors across Dublin and the wider country. Established a solid track record operating across both UK and Irish regulatory frameworks.

Capability
Full 3D Design Across the Team

Invested in full 3D modelling capability across the entire team. From this point, every project is issued with both detailed 2D construction drawings and a complete 3D model — giving everyone involved full clarity before a tube is lifted.

Today
Node Group — Saxon House, London E17

Operating from Walthamstow with a team of specialist engineers delivering scaffold, hoist and MCWP design across heritage, commercial, residential and infrastructure sectors throughout the UK and Ireland.
