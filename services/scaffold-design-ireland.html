Scaffold Design
Across Ireland

Node Group delivers specialist scaffold design services across the Republic of Ireland and Northern Ireland. Full 2D drawings, 3D models and structural calculations on every project — designed from our London studio, delivered to Irish sites.

Why Irish Contractors Choose Node Group

Ireland's scaffold design market is underserved by specialist consultancies. Most Irish scaffold contractors either design in-house or rely on UK firms with limited understanding of Irish site conditions, regulatory frameworks and construction practices. Node Group bridges that gap.

We have direct experience working within Irish regulatory frameworks and have delivered scaffold designs for some of the country's most sensitive heritage buildings. Our engineers understand the differences between UK and Irish standards — from wind loading calculations to local authority requirements — and design accordingly.

Every project receives the same deliverables regardless of location: detailed 2D construction drawings, a complete 3D model and full structural calculations. You speak directly to the engineer designing your scaffold — no sales desk, no call centre.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Node Group work in Ireland?+

Yes. Node Group has an active track record in Ireland, including major heritage and residential projects in Dublin and beyond. We have direct experience working within Irish regulatory frameworks and deliver the same standard of 2D drawings, 3D models and structural calculations on every Irish project.

How does working with a London-based consultancy work for Irish projects?+

Scaffold design is primarily a desk-based engineering discipline. We receive the project brief, survey information and site photos remotely, produce the full design package in our London studio, and issue directly to the site team. For complex projects, our engineers attend site in Ireland for surveys and coordination meetings. The process is identical to our UK work.

Do you design to Irish standards?+

Yes. Our engineers have experience with both UK and Irish regulatory frameworks. We design to Eurocode standards with Irish National Annexes where applicable, including Irish wind loading parameters. Our Garda HQ project in Phoenix Park, Dublin, was verified against Irish wind loading requirements specifically.

What types of scaffold projects do you design in Ireland?+

We design across all sectors in Ireland: heritage scaffold for protected structures (including freestanding solutions where ties are not permitted), residential refurbishment scaffold, temporary roofs, commercial access scaffold, and system scaffold packages. Our Irish portfolio includes the Garda HQ in Phoenix Park and Millbrook Hall.
